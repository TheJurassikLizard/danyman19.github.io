What is FTT?

FTT is a computer CTF (capture the flag) targeted towards windows.
It stands for Find The Thing because we aren't feeling very creative.

We would recommend you have knowledge in batch files
and a basic knowledge in computers.

Particpating is completely free and anonymous.
It is targeted towards middle schoolers from grades 5 to 8.
